Day 3: A Birthday

Saturday 4/10/2021

Listened to Wendy's full album for the first time. She is one of the member's of Red Velvet, a popular Kpop group. This is her first solo album.

Red Velvet

Red Velvet is a South Korean girl group formed by SM entertainment. Debuted in 2014, they originally had four members: Irene, Seulgi, Wendy and Joy. In 2015, they added a fifth member Yeri. They're most famous songs include: Dumb Dumb, Red Flavor, Bad Boy, and Psycho. They have been on an unofficial hiatus since December of 2019.


Overall Opinions: It was a very good album and Wendy is an amazing vocalist. Unfortunately, the majority of the songs were ballads which really is not the type of music that I like.
